Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: 26th December 2025

Amaanitvam Foundation takes the utmost care to process donations as per the instructions given by our donors, online and offline. However, we recognize that there might be instances of erroneous donations or technical glitches.

1. General Policy

Donations made to Amaanitvam Foundation are generally non-refundable. As a charitable organization, we utilize the funds for our social welfare projects immediately. However, refunds may be considered in the following specific circumstances:

  • If a double transaction occurred due to a technical server error.
  • If the donor entered the wrong amount (e.g., ₹10,000 instead of ₹1,000) and notifies us immediately.

2. Time Limit for Requests

If you wish to cancel your donation or request a refund, you must notify us within 7 days of making the donation.

Requests received after 7 days will not be entertained as the funds may have already been allocated to a project.

3. 80G Tax Exemption Receipts

Important: If a tax exemption certificate (Form 10BE / 80G Receipt) has already been issued for your donation, we will NOT be able to process a refund under any circumstances, as per Government of India tax laws.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please send an email to amaanitvamfoundation@gmail.com with the subject line: "Refund Request - [Donation ID]".

Your email must include:

  • Date of Donation
  • Amount Donated
  • Transaction ID / Payment Reference Number
  • Reason for Refund

5. Processing Time

Once a request is received and approved, the refund process will be initiated. The amount will be refunded to the original payment method (Bank Account, Credit Card, or UPI) used during the transaction.

Please allow 5-10 working days for the bank to credit the amount back to your account.
